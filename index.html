Anna Trevisan is a fashion writer and researcher based in the Netherlands and rooted in Italy, whose practice lingers beneath the surface. Her work explores how gendered beauty standards are applied onto the skin to then get underneath it, reaching the subject within. Grounded in technofeminist thought and attuned by the tension between digital and print, her practice extends its research and the writing surfacing from it into independent publishing, treating both the body and the page as political sites.
